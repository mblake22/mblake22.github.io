Specifications

  • Twin engines with power rating from 522 kW to 1040 kW.
  • Two-blade and four-blade rotor variants.
  • Steel lift beams.
  • 42 and 90-degree gearboxes.
  • Tubular landing skids.
  • 2 Pilot seats.
  • 13 crew seats.
  • Rescue hoist and room for 6 strechers.
  • 7.62mm machine guns, miniguns, and unguided rockets.
